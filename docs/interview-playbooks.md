# Interview Playbooks

## Playbook 1: Stalled deal recovery
- Input: late-stage opportunity with budget and timeline objections.
- Agent goal: diagnose blockers and propose a negotiation-ready talk track.
- Success: actionable next meeting plan with explicit risk flags.

## Playbook 2: Pricing objection handling
- Input: procurement pushing heavy discounting.
- Agent goal: preserve value framing while advancing commercial process.
- Success: clear value-defense narrative and next qualification question.

## Playbook 3: Discovery qualification
- Input: early call with unclear pain and weak stakeholder map.
- Agent goal: coach rep toward qualification depth and decision clarity.
- Success: targeted discovery questions tied to sales methodology.
